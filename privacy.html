Privacy comes first. Finance Manager does not sell users' personal or financial information and does not use finance records for third-party behavioural advertising.
01

Introduction

Finance Manager is a personal finance management application designed to help users record and manage their income, expenses, bills, budgets, debts, savings, recurring items, calendar entries, and receipt information.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the application processes, why it is processed, how it is protected, and what choices users have regarding their information.

02

Information We Process

Depending on the features used, Finance Manager may process:

  • Email address used to create and authenticate an account.
  • Income, expense, bill, budget, savings, and debt records.
  • Friend due and family-sharing information entered by the user.
  • Merchant names, transaction dates, amounts, currencies, categories, payment methods, notes, and tax values.
  • Bill or receipt images voluntarily captured or selected by the user.
  • Receipt information extracted using optical character recognition.
  • App preferences such as default currency, app-lock status, and auto-lock timeout.
  • Cloud-sync state and technical information necessary to operate the application.
03

How Information Is Used

User information is used to provide app functionality, including:

  • Creating and authenticating the user's account.
  • Saving and synchronizing personal finance records.
  • Calculating dashboards, reports, budgets, bills, debts, and savings.
  • Extracting receipt details from images selected by the user.
  • Displaying receipt images and associated transaction details.
  • Protecting the app with Face ID, Touch ID, or device authentication.
  • Maintaining app reliability, security, and user preferences.
04

Account and User Data Isolation

The user's email address is used for authentication. Personal finance data is associated with the user's Firebase Authentication user identifier.

User-specific cloud data is stored under an account-specific path and is intended to be accessible only to that authenticated user.

Finance Manager does not send a user's income, expenses, bills, or other finance records to the user's email inbox.
05

Local Device Storage

Finance Manager uses Apple storage technologies, including SwiftData, to store finance records and related information locally on the user's device.

Temporary files may also be stored locally, including downloaded receipt images, OCR images, generated reports, and cache files. These temporary files may be removed using the Clear Cache feature.

06

Firebase Services

Finance Manager uses Firebase services provided by Google:

  • Firebase Authentication for account sign-in and authentication.
  • Cloud Firestore for saving and synchronizing user finance records and settings.
  • Firebase Storage for storing receipt or bill images uploaded by the user.

Cloud records and stored files are organized using the authenticated user's account identifier.

07

Receipt Scanning and OCR

When a user captures or selects a receipt image, Finance Manager may analyze the image to identify information such as:

  • Merchant name
  • Purchase date
  • Total amount
  • Tax amount
  • Currency
  • Receipt items
  • Payment information

Users should review extracted information before saving it because OCR results may occasionally be incomplete or inaccurate.

08

Camera and Photo Library

Finance Manager requests camera access only when the user chooses to photograph a bill or receipt.

Photo-library access is requested only when the user chooses to select an existing receipt or bill image.

The app does not browse or upload unrelated photos without the user's action.

09

Face ID, Touch ID, and Device Authentication

Finance Manager may use Apple's LocalAuthentication framework to protect access to the application.

Biometric and device-passcode verification is handled by iOS. Finance Manager does not receive, collect, or store the user's Face ID data, Touch ID data, or device passcode.

10

Data Sharing and Selling

Finance Manager does not sell users' personal or financial data.

Finance Manager does not use users' finance records for third-party behavioural advertising.

Information may be processed by service providers such as Firebase only as necessary to provide authentication, synchronization, storage, security, and related app functionality.

11

Data Security

Reasonable technical safeguards are used to help protect user data. These safeguards include account-based access control, Firebase security rules, app-lock functionality, authenticated cloud access, and local privacy protection.

No method of electronic storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

12

Data Retention

User information is retained while the account remains active or while it is needed to provide requested app functionality.

Users may delete individual records using available deletion controls inside the app.

13

Account Deletion

Users can delete their account from the Settings section of Finance Manager.

Account deletion is intended to remove:

  • The Firebase Authentication account
  • Associated Cloud Firestore finance records
  • Associated Firebase Storage receipt and bill images
  • Local SwiftData finance records
  • Account-specific preferences and caches

The user may be required to reauthenticate before account deletion can be completed.

14

Sign Out

When a user signs out, Finance Manager clears local finance records, account-specific preferences, authentication information, and receipt-related caches from the device to help prevent another account from viewing the previous user's information.

15

Children's Privacy

Finance Manager is not intended for children under 13 years of age. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

16

International Users

Finance Manager may be used by people in different countries. Cloud information may be processed in locations where Firebase or its service providers operate.

17

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when app functionality, data-processing practices, or legal requirements change.

The updated policy will be published on this webpage with a revised effective date.
